Padhle AIM720 Batch for Droppers: What Is Included and Is It Worth It

Published on July 23, 2026 | Coaching

As a NEET dropper, choosing the right coaching program is one of the most critical decisions you'll make. The Padhle AIM720 batch has emerged as a popular choice among droppers preparing for their second or subsequent NEET attempts. This comprehensive guide breaks down exactly what's included in the program, its structure, cost-effectiveness, and whether it's genuinely worth your investment.

What Exactly Is Padhle AIM720?

Padhle AIM720 is a premium batch designed specifically for NEET droppers who are aiming to score 720+ marks on the exam. Unlike general batches catering to both first-timers and repeaters, AIM720 focuses exclusively on high-performing students who want to refine their preparation and push toward the top percentile. The program operates both online and offline, making it accessible to droppers regardless of their geographical location.

The batch name itself reflects the target score—720 marks out of 720 is the ideal goal. However, realistically, scoring 700+ puts you in an excellent position for top medical colleges across India. Padhle positions this batch as an intensive program that demands serious commitment and consistent effort from dropper students.

Core Components Included in AIM720 Batch

1. Comprehensive Subject Coverage with Advanced Depth

The AIM720 batch covers all three NEET subjects—Physics, Chemistry, and Biology—but with significantly deeper content than standard coaching programs. For droppers, this is crucial because you're no longer learning concepts from scratch; instead, you're refining understanding and tackling advanced problem-solving patterns.

Physics: The program includes advanced mechanics, optics, electromagnetism, and quantum mechanics. Droppers often struggle with numerical problems and concept application, which Padhle addresses through intensive problem-solving sessions. Each topic includes multiple difficulty levels, allowing you to progress from fundamental to Olympiad-level questions.

Chemistry: Organic chemistry receives special attention, with detailed reaction mechanisms, synthesis planning, and named reactions covered exhaustively. Physical chemistry's mathematical aspects are reinforced, and inorganic chemistry's periodic trends and structure-property relationships are explained through visual aids and interactive sessions.

Biology: Both botany and zoology are covered with emphasis on diagram-based learning and conceptual clarity. The program recognizes that biology requires memorization paired with understanding, and provides memory aids, flowcharts, and connections between different topics.

2. Live Interactive Classes and Recorded Content

The AIM720 batch operates a hybrid model where droppers attend live classes conducted by experienced faculty, with sessions recorded for revision. For droppers juggling multiple responsibilities or dealing with inconsistent internet, this flexibility is invaluable.

Live classes are typically scheduled at fixed times, allowing you to interact with instructors, ask doubts, and engage with peer droppers. The recorded versions can be watched at your own pace, making it possible to revisit complex topics immediately after class or during revision phases. Most students find watching lectures at 1.5x speed during revision particularly helpful.

Pro Tip for Droppers

Don't just passively watch lectures. Take notes during live sessions, then use recorded videos for clarification and deeper understanding. This combination maximizes learning and retention for competitive exam preparation.

3. Extensive Question Bank and Practice Materials

AIM720 provides an extensive database of practice questions organized by topic, difficulty level, and exam pattern. For NEET droppers, this is where significant value lies. The question bank includes:

  • Topic-wise questions (basic to advanced levels)
  • Chapter-wise mock tests mimicking NEET pattern
  • Full-length mock exams (typically 5-6 per month)
  • Previous years' NEET papers with detailed solutions
  • Questions based on latest NEET trends and difficulty patterns

For droppers specifically, having access to 2-3 years of actual exam papers plus hundreds of similar-difficulty practice questions accelerates your problem-solving speed and accuracy. Many droppers find that practicing through the AIM720 question bank significantly improves their time management during the actual exam.

4. Personalized Doubt Resolution

The batch includes a structured doubt-resolution system. For droppers who've already studied the material once, having quick access to doubt clearing—rather than sitting through complete topic lectures—is essential. This typically includes:

  • Discussion forums monitored by faculty
  • Doubt sessions scheduled 2-3 times weekly
  • WhatsApp/Telegram groups for peer discussions
  • Video explanations for commonly-asked doubts

Psychological Support and Motivation

The AIM720 batch includes periodic motivation sessions, success stories from previous droppers, and access to a community of like-minded students. Why This Matters for Droppers

First-timers often revise everything superficially. Droppers benefit from targeted revision of only problematic areas. The analytics-driven approach in AIM720 makes this possible, potentially saving 200+ hours of unnecessary study time.
